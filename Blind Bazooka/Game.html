Welcome to Blind Bazooka, this is a turn based game in which each player is given a 15x15 grid to walk around on and start with 10 health. Each player chooses a spot on the opposing player's grid to place a bomb on. The bomb deals damage based on proximity to the player. Each bomb represents a 5x5 grid with the bomb at the center. If the player is at the bomb, they will take 3 damage. If the player is 1 space away from the bomb, they will take 2 damage. If the player is 2 spaces away from the bomb, they will take 1 damage. The last player remaining wins!

Make your move Player 1, Player 1 HP: 10 Player 2 HP: 10
